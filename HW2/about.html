About Rent a Bird

We are a rental service for all sorts of birds and bird acccessories including toucans, flamingos, and penguins among others! Rent a Bird was founded by Brooke Peterson in 2019, and is rapidly growing everyday. Our client base consists of animal--specifically bird--lovers who don't want the lifelong responsibility of owning one.
